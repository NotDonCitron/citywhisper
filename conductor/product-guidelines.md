# Product Guidelines: CityWhisper

## 🎨 Design & Style
- **Aesthetic:** Modern, "Alive," and Polished.
- **Theme:** Dark Mode by default.
- **Accents:** Neon Orange and Teal (high contrast).
- **Typography:** Modern Sans-Serif (e.g., Inter, Montserrat).
- **Feedback:** Interactive transitions and clear UI response (micro-interactions).

## 🖋️ Content & Tone
- **Voice:** Professional yet engaging; "The friendly local guide."
- **Clarity:** Keep narrations concise (under 60s per POI).
- **Ethics:** Neutral information on sensitive topics (e.g., cannabis laws). No promotion of controlled substances.
- **Accuracy:** All AI-generated audio must be based on verifiably true data (RAG).

## 📱 User Interaction
- **Minimalism:** Do not clutter the screen while the user is walking.
- **Accessibility:** Audio narrations must have text transcripts.
- **Seamlessness:** Auto-play audio at POIs (Geofencing) to keep the phone in the pocket.

## ⚖️ Compliance & Safety
- **Legal:** Strictly follow the Dutch "AHOJG-Kriterien" for coffee shops (no advertising).
- **Privacy:** Minimize tracking; only collect essential location data for the tour duration.
- **Sustainability:** Encourage visits to less-crowded areas to support "Tourism in Balance."
