# Product Definition: CityWhisper

## 🎯 Vision
"Die Stadt flüstert dir ihre Geheimnisse zu."
CityWhisper is a personalized city experience app that breaks through traditional thematic silos. It combines diverse user interests—ranging from high art and fine dining to local subcultures and hidden gems—into one seamless, AI-optimized, and narrated walking route.

## 🚀 Core Value Proposition
- **Hyper-Personalization:** Routes are generated on-the-fly based on *specific* user interests (e.g., "Rijksmuseum + best Ramen + History of Cannabis").
- **Smart Navigation:** Algorithmic route optimization (solving the TSP) ensures an efficient path without zigzagging.
- **Dynamic Storytelling:** GPS-triggered, AI-generated audio narrations (RAG-based) provide context-aware information at every POI.
- **Authenticity:** Focus on local "hidden gems" and non-standard tours.

## 👥 Target Audience
- **Primary:** Tech-savvy Millennials and Gen Z travelers who value independence and flexibility over standard group tours.
- **Secondary:** Digital nomads and locals looking for new ways to experience their own city.

## 💎 Key Features
1. **Preference Selection:** Users choose categories like Art, Food, Nightlife, History, Nature, and Shopping.
2. **AI Route Generation:** Real-time calculation of the optimal path based on chosen interests.
3. **Location-Based Audio:** Automatic playback of narrations when entering a POI's geofence.
4. **Offline Capability:** Downloadable maps and audio for international travelers.
5. **Ethical Subculture Info:** Neutral, fact-based information on sensitive topics (e.g., Dutch coffee shop culture).

## 📈 Market Context (Mannheim Focus)
- **Problem:** Standard-Stadtführungen in Mannheim sind oft statisch; es fehlt ein Tool, das Quadrate-Logik, industrielle Geschichte und moderne Gastronomie (z.B. Jungbusch) verbindet.
- **Opportunity:** Positionierung als smarter Begleiter für "Local Experiences" in Mannheim.
- **Monetization:** Affiliate commissions (lokale Partner), Freemium (Premium audio/offline).

## 📍 Beispiel-POIs (Mannheim)
- **Wasserturm & Anlage:** Historische Fakten und Architektur.
- **Schloss Mannheim:** Barocke Geschichte.
- **Jungbusch:** Subkultur, Nachtleben und Gentrifizierung.
- **Quadrate:** Die einzigartige Stadtstruktur erklären.
